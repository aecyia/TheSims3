Description		Aeternum is a well-learned, intelligent, knowledgeable, and well-traveled man of many experiences. He serves the main household with great pride, loyalty, love, and care. He does many tasks including: cleaning the house, does the laundry, washing dishes, serving delicious food of the highest quality, buys food and other essentials, tends to the garden, watering plants, making beds, fending off obsessive fans, caring for babies and children, tutoring children, feeding and cleaning up after pets, protecting the main family from harm ( such as fires, burglars, and other life-threatening situations ), repairing broken objects and electronics, evicting party crashers, entertain party guests with their musical talent and skills, interacting with Sims, giving wondrous insights and advice, answers house phones, and much more. He is the butler, maid, gardener, repairman, nanny, deliveryman, and bodyguard all at once.
